Libz Reader

Welcome to Libzreader!

Libzreader is my take on a classic word game that I enjoyed playing as a kid. Follow the word prompts to build your own wacky story, then click the "READ" button to have your device read your story aloud to you!

This project uses Speech Synthesis from the Web Speech API and an external API from github.com/HermanFassett/madlibz for the stories and prompts.
